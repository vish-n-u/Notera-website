Privacy Policy

Your Privacy, Your Control

Last updated: May 2025

Our Commitment

Notera is built with your privacy in mind. We minimize data collection while providing essential functionality.

Anonymous Authentication

We create an anonymous user profile through Firebase Authentication to:

  • Provide a seamless experience without requiring personal information
  • Your anonymous profile contains no personally identifiable information

Important: If you delete the app or clear app data, your anonymous profile will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience:

  • We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how features are used
  • Technical information helps us identify and fix issues

We never collect or associate any personal information with analytics data.

Audio Processing

  • Audio files are temporarily sent to our secure backend for transcription
  • Audio files are immediately deleted after transcription is complete
  • Transcribed text is stored only on your local device

Data Deletion Warning

Please be aware of the following:

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not maintain backups of your data
  • If you delete the app, clear app data, or delete notes, they will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered
