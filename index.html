Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hands-free note taking with Notera

What is hands-free note taking?

Hands-free note taking means capturing notes without typing. Notera records system audio from other apps on your Android phone, then uses AI to transcribe or summarize it into formatted notes — perfect for when you're on a crowded commute and can't type.

Can I take notes during my commute without typing?

Yes! Notera is designed for commute note taking. Just tap record while listening to podcasts, videos, or any audio on your phone. Notera captures the system audio in the background and transcribes it into well-formatted notes — no typing required.

Does Notera record my microphone or conversations?

No. Notera only records internal system audio from other apps on your phone — not your microphone or live conversations. It captures audio from apps like YouTube, podcasts, or video players that allow system audio capture.

How does Notera work on apps that block audio recording?

Some apps block internal audio capture. For those apps, Notera lets you import video or audio files directly. It then generates formatted notes from the imported media, so you still get your notes.

Is Notera free to use?

Yes, Notera is free to download and use on Android. You can get it on the Google Play Store and start taking hands-free notes immediately.

What makes Notera different from other note taking apps?

Unlike traditional note taking apps that require you to type, Notera lets you take notes hands-free by recording system audio from other apps. It adds a floating overlay window so you can set headers and take screenshots while recording. The AI then creates rich formatted notes with your screenshots embedded at the exact positions you took them.
